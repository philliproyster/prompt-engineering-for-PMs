How To Do It: Core Best Practices

Clarity, Specificity, Context

Provide clear, specific, and context-rich prompts. Avoid ambiguity.

Example:

Instead of: "Summarize financial report."

Try: "Act as a Senior PM... summarize the Q4 2023 financial report focusing on revenue, expenses, and profitability... for an executive audience..."

Iterative Refinement

Start with a simple prompt, review the output, and refine it with more detail.

Example:

Initial: "Generate user stories for expense tracking."

Refinement: "Generate user stories for expense tracking... for construction site supervisors... including their current pain points and acceptance criteria for mobile receipt capture."

Role-Based Prompting

Instruct the model to "Act as a [role]" to adopt a specific perspective and tone.

Example:

Instead of: "Write a marketing email."

Try: "Act as a Product Marketing Manager specializing in construction accounting... Draft a compelling email to mid-market general contractors... Emphasize a 30% reduction in manual reconciliation time..."

Practical Applications Across the Product Lifecycle

Enhancing Discovery & Research

Accelerate understanding of customer pain points and market opportunities.

  • Synthesize customer feedback from Gong & NotebookLM to identify recurring themes and pain points.
  • Identify emerging market opportunities based on economic forecasts.
  • Perform competitive analysis by comparing product launches, pricing, and features.

Streamlining Requirements & Roadmap

Draft initial requirements, refine user stories, and break down large initiatives.

  • Generate JIRA-ready user stories with personas, actions, benefits, and acceptance criteria.
  • Refine requirements to be SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
  • Propose high-level, phased roadmaps for strategic objectives with clear customer value.

Optimizing GTM & Launch

Create compelling marketing collateral and internal enablement materials.

  • Draft GTM strategies including target personas, value propositions, and marketing channels.
  • Generate sales enablement content like talking points and objection handling for Salesforce.
  • Draft concise, customer-facing announcements for new feature releases on Slack.

Streamlining Communication

Facilitate cross-functional alignment and ensure shared understanding.

  • Summarize meeting decisions and action items for Slack and JIRA.
  • Draft concise, high-level updates for executive leadership on project progress and impact.
  • Create inspiring messages for engineering and UX teams to connect their work to strategic outcomes.

Key Recommendations

1

Establish a Centralized Prompt Library: Create a shared, version-controlled repository for reusable prompts.

2

Mandate "AI-as-Calculator": For financials, instruct AI to write and execute code for all precise calculations.

3

Integrate into Workflows: Embed prompt engineering into daily tools like JIRA, Salesforce, and Slack.

4

Cultivate a "Compliance-by-Design" Mindset: Train PMs to proactively include regulatory requirements in prompts.

5

Foster Iterative Experimentation: Encourage continuous prompt refinement and establish feedback loops.
